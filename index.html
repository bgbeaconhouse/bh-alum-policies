Delete Your BH Alum Account

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your BH Alum account directly within the app:

  1. Open the BH Alum app
  2. Go to your Posts
  3. Tap "Support"
  4. Tap "Delete my Account"

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, username)
  • All your posts and comments
  • All your messages and conversations
  • All uploaded photos and videos
  • All account preferences and settings

Alternative: Email Request

If you're unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing:

bgbeaconhouse@gmail.com

Please include your username and email address in the request.

Data Retention

Once deleted, your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.
